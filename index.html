Why PikeForFun?

  1. Because we are promoting :
  2. Nature
  3. Mindfullness
  4. Fun
  5. Our youth pike-fishing club was created as a distraction alternative from the everyday interactions with screens and media in general.
  6. Its purpose is to encourage sportmanship, integrity, conservation and development of new skills and knowledge.
  7. Senior guests (over 20s) are welcome to engage in our activities for guidance and support.
  8. Our club meetings will usually take place every saturday between 10 am and 3 pm.
